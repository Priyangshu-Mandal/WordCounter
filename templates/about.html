About Us

This simple website counts the number of words in the text that you have entered. Though this may seem childish, this website can help you a lot. It can count the number of words in an article that you must prepare, thus saving the time that you would have taken to count. Plus, the accuracy. For a big text, you may lose the count during counting and get a wrong number of words. But this will not happen with Word Counter. All you have to do is enter the text, click on Submit, and BOOM! Their is the number of words in your text in front of you in no time!
